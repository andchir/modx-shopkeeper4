Shopkeeper 4 for MODX
=====================

Snippet to display categories, products, filters, bread crumbs, etc. from the MongoDB database.
For this extra to work, Shopkeeper 4 (https://github.com/andchir/shopkeeper4) must be installed
on your or remote server. For the shopping cart you need to use the snippet
Shopping Cart (https://github.com/andchir/modx-shopping-cart) (now in development).

* Documentation: https://github.com/andchir/modx-shopkeeper4/wiki
* Official site: https://modx-shopkeeper.ru/
